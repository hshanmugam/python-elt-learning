# Topic 15 Mini Quiz

1. What does a dimension table mainly store?
   Answer: Descriptive business attributes
2. What does a fact table mainly store?
   Answer: Measurable business events or metrics
3. Why is staging useful?
   Answer: It prepares and standardizes source data before curated modeling
4. What is one common incremental load rule?
   Answer: Load only data newer than the last processed timestamp
5. Why are curated tables valuable?
   Answer: They are easier for analytics and reporting consumers to use
