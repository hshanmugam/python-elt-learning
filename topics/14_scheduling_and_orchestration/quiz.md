# Topic 14 Mini Quiz

1. What does scheduling answer?
   Answer: When a job should run
2. What does orchestration add beyond scheduling?
   Answer: Dependencies, retries, monitoring, and workflow control
3. Why is idempotency important?
   Answer: It helps repeated runs avoid duplicate or corrupt results
4. What is one common orchestration platform?
   Answer: Airflow
5. Why do scheduled pipelines need alerts?
   Answer: So teams know quickly when something goes wrong
