# Topic 10 Mini Quiz

1. What is the main table structure in pandas?
   Answer: `DataFrame`
2. Which method is used to join two DataFrames?
   Answer: `merge`
3. What does `groupby` help with?
   Answer: Aggregating rows by key columns
4. How do you check for missing values?
   Answer: With methods like `isna()`
5. Why is pandas popular in ETL?
   Answer: It makes tabular data transformation fast and expressive
